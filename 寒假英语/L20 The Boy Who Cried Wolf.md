# L20 The Boy Who Cried Wolf

#### 正文

    Boy: I can see a wolf!
    Men: We can't see a wolf!
    Boy: I can see a big wolf!
    Men: We can't see a big wolf!
    Boy: I can see a big, bad wolf!
    Men: We can't see a big, bad wolf!
